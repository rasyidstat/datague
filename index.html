Data Gue

Welcome to Data Gue!

There is a lot of data that I can visualize out there. However, I use my own data as a resource to level up my data wrangling and data visualization skills. I feel more motivated when I use my own data for data visualization. That’s why I open Data Gue, my personal side project, mainly about visualizing my own data, as well as a place for me to express myself with data. It is like personal analytics or quantified-self.

Here are what I would like to look from my own data:

  • Lunch
  • Transportation (GO-JEK)
  • Twitter
  • Book that I read
  • Flight

I ask myself again whether it is really dangerous or not to open my own personal data. I know the term of data privacy is important in this nowadays age even many people in my country, Indonesia, do not really care with that. That’s why I will never share my detailed data here. I only show the aggregated and visualized version.

Once again, welcome to Data Gue!
